Calcium and magnesium is best taken in a ratio of 2:1 in a dissolved acidic state. We achieve this by adding apple cider vinegar. Calcium needs an acidic environment to be absorbed in the intestines, but with magnesium added, the environment becomes alkaline. Apple cider vinegar provides the acidic environment and therefore good absorption.
